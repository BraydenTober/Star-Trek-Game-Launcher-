Star Trek: Survival

Survive as many ships as possible, while collecting credits and upgrading your ship! You can switch between 3 factions, which each have their own skills! Fly the new DISCOVERY and use it's spore drive, or cloak as a KLINGON bird of prey!

SOUND EFFECTS: YES

Away Team 9

Help the ambushed station recover it's research, stolen by KLINGON leader T'Kuvma. Complete puzzles to advance -- and a difficult FINAL BOSS BATTLE against T'Kuvma himself!

SOUND EFFECTS: NO
